Soulmate & Artifact

Ranked based on Damage Potential

Endgame

Name Information
Lugman - The current BiS Soulmate for Raid Contents due to the high stat provided.
Neb - Less stat than Lugman, how much worse it is depends on which weapon’s overclocking level you at.
Miriam - Increase 10 SV per 10 seconds (only in SSS tier).
- Not a good pet for Damage Potential since Dhana (or any 2nd Advanced characters) don't need SV for EX Skill
Catherine - Has decent stat.
- Instantly resurrect in mazes (90 seconds cooldown). This is really useful in “unable to revive” contents like Advancing Helix.
Arua - Has decent stat.
- Recover 5% stamina per second. Very useful in the new Lugman raid for new players suffering from lacking stamina.

Other pets are not worth using. (Unnecessary and lower stat compared to listed Soulmates above)

Early Game

Name Information
Neb - Easy and powerful Soulmate you can get in the Early Phase of the game.
- Better than from S to SS tier. (You can craft 1 Neb box for 12 Desire Fragment of Lost Light in Arcadia Realm - Sedurin)
Catherine - Give similar stat to Neb.
- Instantly resurrect in mazes without taking a self resurrection limit (120 seconds cooldown).
Lugman - Give less stat than the two above but you will need to grind Lugman anyways since she is the strongest Soulmate when she reaches SSS tier.
Arua - Decent choice due to high Armor Break.
Chii - Best A-Base tier Soulmate.

NameInformation
