Terminology

(My own definition for better explanation, not game definition)

Word Name Meaning Which Skill
Invincible Able to block most kinds of damage and negative buffs. Finis Temporis, Desire Awakening, Ars Nova: Mandala
Iframe Able to negate damage AND some boss patterns but can’t avoid fatal patterns. Dodge, Tumble, Retaliation
Absolute Invincible Block all damage and negative buffs - Can’t skip mech (Rugman only). Dodge (Good Timing), Soul Evasion, Orbital Blade
